Outreach

In addition to my interest in industry, broadening participation and access to computer science education is another one of my passions. For the past two years, I have been serving as an Ambassador Lead with the Paul G. Allen School of Computer Science, working to promote diversity within the school through intentional community outreach.

Throughout my time as an ambassador, I have visited over 30 schools, led conversations in local communities about tech, and engaged a countless number of enthusiastic students to answer their questions--what does "tech" really mean? Who represents tech? How they can make tech a part of their own lives? In the summer of 2020 I also had the opportunity to teach a six-week introductory programming class as a part of the University of Washington's Upward Bound Math Science program, a federal TRIO program.
