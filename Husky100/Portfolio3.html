Aikido

Aikido was in many ways the first club I joined at UW. I joined in Spring quarter 2016, and started as a white belt. Now, I am not that much farther belt-wise, but I never thought I would be club president so soon either.



As club president, I intend to make better use of the resources available to us as an RSO, as well as lay a strong foundation for future officers to come. My previous year, I helped to open our club bank account as the Club Treasurer. This year I will establish a club budget with the IMA, help with the standardization of the attendence system, refit our stocks of training equipment, and redefine/clarify the officer roles. I hope to be able to create a strong officer group for this club so that we will be prepared when future opportunities open up!
