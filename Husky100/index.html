Hi There. I'm Alex!

I am a junior currently studying computer science. I live life with the desire to inspire others to promote positve change in their communities. I believe that everybody's voice and stories have a right to be heard. And today, with the advances in technology that we have, there is no reason for anybody to be silenced. On paper, my Husky Story may have started in September 2015, but in many ways I feel it is only just beginning now. I did not make the choice to be truly involved within my community until Junior year, and because of that, my 2017 Fall Quarter has been one of my most challenging, yet rewarding, quarters thus far.


So what did I do? Well, this fall quarter I dove in and started pursuing my hobbies and interests at a new depth. In my stories below, you'll hear from inside the amazing educational outreach team I work with, attend pit-orchestra practice sessions for the musical 'The Addams Family', enter Aikido club and see how its officers are establishing a stronger foundation for the future, and you'll visit my first apartment to get a glimpse at what living independently has been for me.


Not all change comes easily though, and that holds true especially for me. While I invested my time in many new hobbies, I also struggled with my career, trying to align my studies with my guiding principles. I struggled to find motivation in my classes, and frequently asked myself if I was ever in the right place. I questioned the example given to me, the path that most were expected to follow, and I started to slowly pave a new path for myself. After my stories, I will reflect on what I have learned from each of them, and where I feel those experiences will take me.

What's next?

I will continue serving in my educational outreach position, in order to empower and inspire younger students. I will continue to carry my guiding principles with me, and apply them in any capacity I can. I will continue to make the most of the opportunities I am provided here. I will continue to contribute to both Stage Notes and Aikido, no matter what position I hold within those RSOs. I will study abroad, and learn about the world from another culture. I will find my own story, understand it, share it, and then seek to learn the stories of others.


But above all else, I will never cease to be a student, and never cease to stop learning from those around me.
